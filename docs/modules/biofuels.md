# Biofuels

The upgraded GEMINI-E3-EU model incorporates biofuels as an energy source for electricity generation using updated data from the GTAP Power 11 database and BP Statistical Review. Biofuel electricity generation is represented across the model’s regional aggregation framework, ensuring consistency between supply and demand through market-clearing conditions.

The model includes estimates of production costs, Levelized Cost of Electricity (LCOE), and associated CO₂ emissions for biofuel-based power generation. In addition to electricity production, the framework also considers broader biofuel supply, consumption, and trade dynamics using data from the International Energy Agency (IEA).
