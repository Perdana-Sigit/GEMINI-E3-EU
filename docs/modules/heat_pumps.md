# Heat Pumps

The upgraded GEMINI-E3-EU model improves the representation of heat pump technologies, reflecting their growing role in low-carbon energy transitions. The model explicitly incorporates air-to-air heat pumps, which are widely used in residential and commercial buildings and offer substantial emissions reductions compared to conventional fossil-fuel-based heating systems.

Heat pump performance is represented using the Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio (SEER), which measures the amount of useful heating energy produced per unit of electricity consumed. The model also integrates Eurostat and other statistical data to estimate electricity consumption associated with heat pump use across sectors.

The representation of heat pumps is embedded within the nested CES framework governing household energy consumption, allowing substitution between heat pumps and alternative heating technologies in response to changes in relative prices and climate policy measures.
