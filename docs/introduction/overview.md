# Overview

## What is GEMINI-E3-EU?

GEMINI-E3-EU is a recursive dynamic, multi-country, multi-sector Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) model designed to analyse interactions between the economy, energy systems, and the environment.

The model simulates the functioning of domestic and international markets under the assumption of market equilibrium, where supply and demand are balanced through adjustments in relative prices, wages, interest rates, and exchange rates.

The model is particularly designed for climate and energy policy analysis, enabling the assessment of greenhouse gas mitigation strategies, carbon pricing mechanisms, energy transitions, and their macroeconomic, sectoral, and distributional implications.

At the core of the model lies a nested Constant Elasticity of Substitution (CES) structure, which allows producers and households to substitute between production factors, energy carriers, and consumption goods in response to changes in relative prices and policy signals.

International trade is represented using the Armington assumption, where domestic and imported goods are treated as imperfect substitutes differentiated by country of origin.

## Model outputs

The model produces annual outputs by country and sector, including:

- Greenhouse gas emissions
- Carbon prices and marginal abatement costs
- Energy production and consumption
- Electricity generation mixes
- Trade flows
- Sectoral production and prices
- Welfare and consumption impacts
- Labour market adjustments
- Regional and distributional indicators
