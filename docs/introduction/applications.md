# Scope and Intended Applications

GEMINI-E3-EU is designed to support a broad range of policy and research applications, including:

- Carbon pricing and Emissions Trading Systems (ETS)
- Energy transition and decarbonisation pathways
- Assessment of European climate policy architectures
- Electricity system transformation
- Industrial decarbonisation,
- Distributional impacts of climate policies
- Labour market transitions
- Air pollution and health co-benefits
- International trade and carbon leakage analysis
- Comparative scenario analysis within Integrated Assessment Modelling frameworks.

The model is particularly suited for medium- and long-term scenario analysis extending to 2050 and beyond.
